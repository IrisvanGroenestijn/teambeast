About us

Values

Roar with Confidence:

A powerful presentation is the backbone of a strong image. With memorable mascots, we empower teams and clubs in confidently and strongly representing who they are.

Smells like TEAM spirit:

We believe in the power of strong teams. A mascot is more than just a symbol; it brings players and supporters together, reinforcing a shared identity.

Fuel the Fire:

Every successful team is made out of unity, enthusiasm, and energy. By creating mascots that enhance team spirit and make every game an exciting experience, we are contributing to fueling the flame.

Game Changer:

We at TeamBeast are THE gamechanger! Aiming to provide a sustainable and budget-friendly way for young amateur sports teams to enhance the collective and empower the team-spirit by providing them with mascots completely tailored to their taste profiles.
