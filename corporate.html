Testing Report

Testing Goals: Our goal is to evaluate the usability and effectiveness of the TeamBeast website by assessing:

  •  How easily users can navigate the site and find key information.
  • Whether the ordering process for custom mascots is clear.
  • How engaging the website’s design and branding feel to our target audience.
  • Any pain points that may cause frustration or prevent users from completing an order. 

    • Testing Methods:

    Participants

  • Number of participants: 8 users
  • Target audience match: Amateeur teamplayers, coaches and team managers who might purchase a mascot.

    • Test Setting

  • Format: A mix of in-person and remote testing.
  • Materials used: website protoype, and screen recording tools
  • Location: conducted on campus

    • Test Protocol:Participants were given specific tasks, such as:

  • Finding information about custom mascots.
  • Placing an order
  • Exploring the homepage and providing feedback on the design

    • They were encouraged to think aloud while completing the tasks. Responses were recorded via screen recordings and notes from observers..
