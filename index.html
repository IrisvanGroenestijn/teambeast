Who we are

At TeamBeast, we design custom mascots for amateur teams, bringing energy, identity, and unforgettable moments to every game.

We believe every team, big or small, deserves a mascot that reflects its spirit. Whether it’s funny, fierce, or unique, we create a mascot that perfectly matches your team’s personality.

Together, we make every game more exciting and strengthen the bond between the team and its fans.



Our vision


We Roar, We Score! A team without spirit is just a group of players. That’s where we come in! TeamBeast brings hype, pride, and power, because every club deserves to stand out, loud and proud!
